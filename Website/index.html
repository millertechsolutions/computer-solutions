Welcome to Miller Tech Solutions!

At Miller Tech Solutions, I specialize in custom-built PCs, computer repairs, and system upgrades. Whether you're a gamer, a professional, or simply need a reliable machine, I’m here to provide expert service and advice.

Our Services

Important Note: I do not supply parts or computers. Customers must bring their own parts and computers for all services.

  • Custom PC Builds

    Expertly crafted PCs tailored to your gaming, work, or personal needs. You bring the parts, and I handle the build!

  • Repairs & Diagnostics

    Quick and reliable diagnostics and fixes for your computer issues. Customers must provide their own parts if replacements are needed.

  • Hardware Upgrades

    Enhance your system's performance with the latest components. Bring your own parts, and I’ll handle the installation.

  • Consultation Services

    Not sure what you need? Get expert advice on parts and builds. Once you’ve decided, bring your components for the service.

How It Works

I strive to make the process of getting your computer built, repaired, or upgraded as smooth and convenient as possible. Here’s how it works:

  • Option 1: Drop-off Bring your computer and any required parts directly to me. I will perform the service and notify you once your machine is ready for pickup.
  • Option 2: On-site Service I can come to your location to work on your computer. This is ideal for quick fixes or if you need help setting up your system.

Note: For on-site services, additional fees may apply depending on your location and the nature of the job.

Pricing Information

  • Basic Service: Starting at $60 for diagnostics and simple fixes.
  • Complex Repairs or Builds: Prices vary based on labor and complexity.

Every project is unique, and I ensure fair pricing based on the job's requirements. Contact me for a personalized quote!
